Overview
Everything your business needs, in one place
LMUC POS is a web-based system that runs in your browser — no installation required. It handles sales, inventory, purchasing, accounting, and reporting for single or multi-branch operations.
🧾

Fast Point of Sale

Touch-optimized POS with barcode scanning, quick product search, table selection, and instant bill printing.

📦

Inventory Control

Real-time stock levels, low-stock alerts, stock movement history, and damage tracking across all branches.

🛒

Purchase Management

Raise purchase orders, receive stock via GRN with partial receive support, and track supplier invoices.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Sales trends, revenue charts, fast-moving items, busiest hours, payment method breakdown, and more.

💰

Accounting

Automatic double-entry journal postings for every sale, purchase, GRN, salary, and expense — no manual entry.

🏪

Multi-Branch

Each branch is fully isolated. Staff see only their branch data; owners and admins see everything.

Modules
Full feature breakdown
Every module is accessible from the sidebar based on the user's role and permissions.
1

Dashboard

Today's revenue, monthly totals, pending bills, low-stock alerts, fast-moving items, revenue trend chart (30 days), payment method donut, busiest hours bar chart, and recent bills table.

2

New Bill / POS

Two-panel POS layout — product card grid on the left with category tabs, barcode scanner, and search; bill builder on the right with table/customer selector, item quantities, discounts, tax, and payment method. Supports draft bills and direct completion.

3

Sales & Invoices

Full sales history with This Week / Today / This Month quick filters, status filter (paid, pending, partial, refunded), search by invoice or customer, and printable receipts.

4

Products

Product catalogue with images (Cloudinary), SKU, barcode, selling price, stock level badges, category and type filters, low-stock filter, and one-click barcode label printing. Image zoom lightbox on click.

5

Categories

Manage product categories per branch. Assign products to categories for faster POS filtering.

6

Purchase Orders

Create and manage purchase orders (POs) with line items, unit costs, and supplier assignment. Expandable rows show full item details per order. Status tracking: pending → partial received → completed.

7

GRN — Goods Received Notes

Receive stock against a PO. Select a PO and all items auto-populate with ordered quantities. Adjust received qty, free qty, unit cost, batch number, and expiry date per line. Stock is incremented immediately on save.

8

Suppliers

Supplier directory with contact details, city, country, and branch assignment. Linked to products and purchase orders.

9

Supplier Returns

Record returns to suppliers with quantity and reason. Stock is automatically decremented and accounting entries are posted.

10

Customers

Customer database with name, phone, email, and loyalty tracking. Customers can be selected at POS for personalised billing and credit tracking.

11

Tables

Table layout management for dine-in orders. Tables can be assigned to bills at the POS and tracked for open orders.

12

Open Bottles

Track partially consumed liquor bottles. When a serving is sold, the system finds an open bottle with enough remaining volume or auto-opens a new one, decrementing stock accordingly.

13

Bottle Deposits

Manage bottle deposit liabilities. Deposits are recorded automatically when a bottle sale is completed and cleared when the bottle is returned.

14

Damage Reports

Log damaged or lost stock with quantity and reason. Stock is decremented and a damage expense entry is posted to the accounting ledger.

15

Stock Ledger

Full audit trail of every stock movement — sales, GRNs, purchases, returns, damages — with timestamps, user, and reference details.

16

Finance

Income and expense tracking, salary records, and the full double-entry accounting journal. View profit/loss by date range.

17

Price Matrix

Manage tiered or bulk pricing rules per product, applicable at the POS during billing.

18

Day End

End-of-day cash reconciliation — record physical cash count, view expected vs. actual, and close the shift with a summary report.

19

Reports

Exportable reports covering sales summary, stock summary, purchase history, customer activity, and financial statements — filterable by date range and branch.

20

Audit Log

Immutable log of all major system events — who created, edited, or deleted records and when. Essential for accountability and compliance.

21

Users & Roles

Manage staff accounts with role-based access control. Assign roles (Owner, Admin, Manager, Cashier, Store Keeper) and branch. Toggle active/inactive status.

22

Restaurant Settings

Configure your shop name, address, city, country, and logo. The logo appears on the login screen and browser tab automatically.

Getting Started
Up and running in minutes
Follow these steps after receiving your system URL from your administrator.
1

Open your system URL

Open the URL provided to you in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari). No installation needed.

2

Log in with your credentials

Use the email and temporary password provided. You will be prompted to change your password after first login.

Email: admin@store.local Password: (provided by your administrator)
3

Configure your restaurant settings

Go to Settings → Restaurant Settings and fill in your business name, address, and upload your logo. This appears on the login screen and receipts.

4

Add your product categories

Go to Products → Categories and create the categories that match your menu (e.g. Liquor, Beer, Food, Soft Drinks).

5

Add your products

Go to Products and click Add Product. Fill in name, SKU, category, selling price, stock quantity, and optionally upload an image.

6

Create your staff accounts

Go to Settings → Users and add your cashiers, managers, and store keepers with their roles and branch assignment.

7

Start taking orders

Click New Bill in the sidebar. Select a table or customer, add products by clicking cards or scanning barcodes, and press Complete Sale.
